About Us

Titan Robotics strives to develop student leaders through working together to create interesting solutions to complex problems. As a dedicated new team, we inspire and motivate each other to become thoughtful, disciplined, and creative thinkers in a professional atmosphere.

Our team is a part of the magnificent FIRST program. FIRST stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology. It was fundamentally created to inspire students of all levels to understand, use, and enjoy science and technology. Today, 90% of FIRST alumni are pursuing a career in STEM fields.

Titan Robotics was founded in 2014 at the Plano, Texas ISD Academy High School. We first participated in the 2015 FRC season. During our first 2 years as a team, we attended the FRC World Championships twice. In the 2015 season, our first year as a team, we had no experience in robotics, few sponsors, and few mentors. That season, we won the Rookie All-Star Award, an award given to the most promising new team at a regional competition. This award is not only for building a winning robot. The team at the Houston Regional

In our second year, 2016, we were finalists at the Dallas and Houston regionals. We won our spot to the World Championships at the Lone Star Regional in Houston. We placed 16th out of 75 in our division at World Championships. Throughout the season, over 50,000 people from across the world watched us compete.
