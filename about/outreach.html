During the offseason of 2016, our team spent time volunteering with the FLL qualifier. We hosted and staffed different events at our school where we witnessed young minds tackle the tasks given to them. The events help them learn more about STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math), and develop teamwork and leadership skills as they use their brainpower and wit to solve the challenges to the best of their abilities and aim to advance to the next competition. While we were there, we also had the opportunity to meet up with the Mindcrafters and the Techno Girls. Two teams for which we helped design team shirts. It was truly valuable experience for the FLL teams and Titan Robotics.
